The Dawn of the Third Age of Mankind

The story begins in the year 2258. It is 10 years after the Earth Minbari war, and a dream has been given form.

Five miles long and home to a quarter of a million humans and aliens, Babylon 5 was located in neutral territory, and with an Ambassador from every major power and many in the League of Non-Aligned Worlds, it was a diplomatic port. It was in fact, our last best hope for peace.
